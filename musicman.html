    Behind History

    One day I was just talking to one of my friends then she asked me to join discord to listen music. At the very beginning I was not familiar with discord. So I was qurious, how we can listen music together. Then she added a bot named Fredboat. It was good but I faced a problem. While listening music at late night, often we fell asleep and the bot continued to play musics in the voice channel.
    Back then, I had wondered if anything with a timer system can be created. So I can add a timer after that the bot will stop playing musics.
    And then I made MusicMan.

    What can MusicMan do?

    It can

    • Play musics from pre-stored playlist
    • Play musics from youtube link
    • Play, Pause, Resume, Stop, Shuffle (Basic Operations)
    • Set a Timer

    How I made it?

    It is written in Python.
    It is hosted in Replit.com (Music Man)
    And to keep it live and monitoring I used Uptimerobot.com
